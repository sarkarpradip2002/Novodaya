ABOUT NAVODAYA

We Create and Turn Into Reality

NAVODAYA, the annual cultural fest of Narula Institute Of Technology (NIT) is just the right concoction of fun for you, a squint of color , a dollop of culture and a dash of mirth. The fest provides a platform for the students to explore their talents and give them recognition, polish different facets of their personalities which weren’t noticed before.
